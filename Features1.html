The Audi A5 2016 contains many features. It can have either a six-speed manual, or eight-speed automatic transmission. It has HID headlights with LED accents, trizone climate control, leather apholstery, and 10-way power front seats. It has bluetooth connectivity, a panoramic glass roof, and a 10-speaker audio system.

Other Features


- Fueled with Unleaded Premium.
- Can seat up to 4 people.
- It can get up to 32 miles per gallon.
- It has 2 doors, and folding seats.
